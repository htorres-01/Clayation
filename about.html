About the Artist

Ellie Reineke is a creative artist, book lover, and a happy mother. When she is not playing with her son Raphael or reading books, she spends a lot of her free time crafting the beautiful clay earrings you see here! Each pair of earrings are carefully hand-molded and baked with perfection in mind. Each earring has a story and and inspiration fueled by Ellie's vivid imagination and shaped by her keep eye for detail. Be it a gift for a loved one or a keepsake for yourself, Ellie wants to make sure your earings are perfect for you.
